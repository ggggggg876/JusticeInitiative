Email a Prisoner

Email any of the political prisoners listed in our registry for free. Just send the Justice Initiative an email with your message, and we will forward it to the prisoner. When a reply is received we will scan and email you the reply. For guidelines as to the content of the message see How to Write a Prisoner.

This service is intended to lower the barrier to entry in corresponding with an inmate. Additionally, this service protects the sender's mail address from being exposed.
