Newsletter

Every two months the Justice-Initiative compiles a newsletter to send out to every one of our prisoners. The newsletter is a compilation of pro-White articles from a variety of sources. Whatever we like and would think the prisoners might like. This is an effort to keep all our prisoners regularly engaged with the pro-White community and the Justice Initiative. We are pleased to have recieved such positive feedback from prisoners on this newsletter.
